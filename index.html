BIO

Magic Nic was not born into a musical home. Sheepishly self-taught, he left a cold town on the New England plateau to find it on the inner-west stages of Sydney. Belting over three part harmonies of garage-pop outfit Easy Street and transcending the astral plane with experimental noise rockers Greyhound, Magic Nic was slowly building a collection of heart sung scripts to croon over his creaking, soulful strokes.

Summoning the ghost of Billie, the spirit of Chet, Bukka's moan and a borrowed electric guitar, he somehow emulsified it all to a warbling cassette tape. Joining him on his upcoming EP are the General Electric, the most frightening rhythm section on this side of the sun.
